 
 
19−22 SEPTEMBER 2023 
CROCUS EXPO, MOSCOW, RUSSIA 
 
World Food Moscow is the largest autumn food exhibition in Russia and the 
CIS countries serving as the main meeting place for industry companies. Food 
and drink market leaders annually present a wide range of agricultural products. 
The event provides an opportunity to demonstrate food products to potential 
partners from Russia and other countries. This year, organizers expect over 
1,000 exhibitors from 30 countries and more than 20,000 visitors from 80 
countries and 80 regions of Russia.  
World Food Moscow consists of 16 main sectors: “Alcohol drinks”; “Grocery”; 
“Soft drinks”; “Frozen, Convenience and Ready-to-Eat Food”;” Confectionary 
and Bakery”; “Canned Food”; “Oils, Fats and Sauces”; “Dairy Products and 
Cheese”; “Meat, Poultry and Eggs”; “Organic and Healthy Food”; “Fish and 
Seafood”; “Fruit and Vegetables”; “Halal”; “Tea and Coffee”; “Non-Food”; 
“Ingredients”. 
The Embassy of Sri Lanka in Russia conveys the invitation to take part with a 
national exposition of industry enterprises in the 32nd International Food and 
Drink Exhibition for Retail and Hotels. World Food Moscow will take place to 
present the food export potential of the country, find new partners, and maintain 
business contacts. In par with national exposition the brand chef from Sri Lanka 
is most welcome to the Culinary marathon to demonstrate the national cuisine to 
the professional audience. The Brochure of the Fair and the Post show review
